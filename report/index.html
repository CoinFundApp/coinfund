February 28, 2022 2022-10-11 3:20

We pride ourselves in allowing decentralized crowdfunding and donations with Bitcoin and Altcoin cryptocurrency through censorship-resistant, self-custody wallets. 

This means we cannot blocklist wallet addresses or access, freeze, or alter any funds within wallets generated by our service.

However, we can block IP addresses as well as blackhole any campaign page that violates our Acceptable Use Policy.

Blocking measures can only be done on our clearnet site. On our decentralized, IPFS mirror we are unable to block IPs or blackhole any campaign URL.

Is there a campaign that breaks our Acceptable Use Policy? Please report it using this form.

     NOTE: We cannot block or ban an IPFS campaign URL
