Privacy policy

September 23, 2020 2022-03-01 6:39

This policy details how data about you is used when you access our CoinFund websites and services (together, “CoinFund”) or interact with us. If we update it, we will revise the date, place notices on CoinFund if changes are material, and/or obtain your consent as required by law.

CoinFund is committed to protecting your privacy. Your Seed Phrase and Private Keys are never transmitted or stored to CoinFund but certain data from CoinFund Wallet are, including logs, error codes, error descriptions, and telemetry data. It is used to improve the application experience and solve technical issues.

Protecting your privacy

  • We do not sell your data to third-parties.
  • We do not share your data with third-parties for marketing purposes.
  • We take precautions to prevent unauthorized access to or misuse of data about you.
  • We do not engage in cross-marketing or link-referral programs.
  • We do not employ tracking devices for marketing purposes.
  • We do not send you unsolicited communications for marketing purposes.
  • Please review privacy policies of any third-party sites linked to from CoinFund.

Data we use to provide/improve our services and/or combat fraud/abuse:

  • data you post on or send via CoinFund, and/or send us directly or via other sites
  • credit card data, which is transmitted to payment processors via a security protocol (e.g. SSL).
  • web log data (e.g. web pages viewed, access times, IP address, HTTP headers).
  • data about your device(s) (e.g. screen size, DOM local storage, plugins).
  • data from analytics (e.g. device type, geo-location via IP address).

Handling of data

  • We retain data as needed for our business purposes and/or as required by law.
  • We make good faith efforts to AES-256 bit encrypt all data, but can make no guarantees.

Circumstances in which we may disclose user data:

  • to vendors and service providers (e.g. payment processors) working on our behalf.
  • to respond to subpoenas, search warrants, court orders, or other legal process.
  • to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of users of CoinFund or the general public.
  • in connection with a merger, bankruptcy, or sale/transfer of assets.
  • in aggregate/summary form, where it cannot reasonably be used to identify you.
