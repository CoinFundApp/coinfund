How to get crypto crowdfunded or donations

March 2, 2022 2022-10-11 2:20
HOW TO START

Getting crypto crowdfunded
or donations

Start a campaign
in a few steps

1. Create a wallet

From our app, select the desired crypto you want to be funded with in your campaign.

2. Save seed phrase

Once the wallet is created, save the seed phrase and click ellipsis to save the private key.

3. Click plus symbol

Next to the wallet of the cryptocurrency click the + symbol to start a new campaign.

4. Fill out details

Enter the amount you want crowdfunded or donated, the campaign title and description.

5. Share the campaign

Copy the link to the campaign's page and share it across all your social media accounts.

6. Get funded

Convince people to fund your campaign by sending funds from their own CoinFundIt.com wallets.
